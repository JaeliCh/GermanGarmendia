On his official store, you’ll find thoughtfully curated collections that include T‑shirts, hoodies, mugs, phone cases, posters, pillow covers, face masks, notebooks, mouse pads, bags, and zipper pouches—many of which are offered as best sellers or new arrivals, often with promotional discounts. At the same time, fan-made contributions, particularly through platforms like Redbubble, expand the options with custom-designed t‑shirts (including graphic, classic, and oversized fits) and high-quality vinyl or holographic stickers showcasing themes such as HolaSoyGerman or JuegaGerman.
